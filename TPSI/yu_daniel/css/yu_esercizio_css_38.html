Instrucions

Preparation:

  • As a group, choose one of the art movements that we discussed and choose one artwork to discuss.

Activity:

  • In the first box, paste your chosen artwork.
  • Look at the artwork then answer the following questions as a group.

1 min:

  • Write your names on the sticky notes.
  • Use them to answer the questions.

10 mins:

  • Write down your answers to the questions in the boxes.
  • These can just be phrases or bullet points.

5 mins:

  • As a group, discuss the answers.
  • Decide which answers you will present to the class.
